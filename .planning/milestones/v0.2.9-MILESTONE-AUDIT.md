### WARNING: Stale Workflow Positions After Session Logging

`useCreatePaintingSession` in `src/hooks/useJournalSessions.ts` invalidates sessions/analytics/activity caches but does NOT invalidate `["workflow-positions"]`. After logging a session, KanbanCard and CurrentFocusCard display stale positions until the 5-minute staleTime expires.

**Fix:** Add `qc.invalidateQueries({ queryKey: ["workflow-positions"] })` to `useCreatePaintingSession`'s `onSuccess` callback.

**Affected requirements:** PROJ-03, PROJ-04
